# Micro-SaaS Factory

QuietPivot Labs is the micro-SaaS lane inside ImonEngine. The generic venture blueprint defines the business shape, but the dedicated launch dossier is produced by the micro-SaaS planner.

## Command

- `npm run dev -- micro-saas-plan --business imon-micro-saas-factory`
- `npm run dev -- micro-saas-plan --business imon-micro-saas-factory --notify-roadblocks`

## What It Generates

- Product backlog with one primary MVP and backup offers.
- Launch calendar covering build, proof, support, and review tasks.
- Social presence registry snapshot and post schedule for the QuietPivot umbrella and niche lanes.
- Paid growth plan for Meta once prerequisites are live.
- Income stack status for payments, hosting, support, analytics, and verified revenue reporting.
- Owner-action roadblocks with exact next steps.
- Legal checklist for manual review before paid launch.

## Expected External Inputs

- Stripe checkout or payment links.
- Real QuietPivot launch domain and hosting target.
- Live QuietPivot social accounts under the scaffolded aliases.
- Meta ad account credentials if paid growth should run.
- Analytics destination such as GA or PostHog.
- Manual legal review before opening paid access.

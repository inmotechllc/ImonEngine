# Social Accounts

## Parent-System Rule

- `ImonEngine` is the parent system name.
- `Imon` is the legacy first-store exception.
- Every future business should use its own public umbrella brand plus one or more plus-tag aliases.

## Umbrella-Brand Policy

- Use Facebook Pages sparingly and strategically.
- Facebook is justified when:
  - the business is scalable enough to share one ad/content surface across multiple niches
  - the business is the public ImonEngine system
  - the business is a physical or Shopify/POD umbrella brand
- When Facebook is justified, create one umbrella Meta asset and one umbrella Facebook Page for the business.
- Do not create one Facebook Page per niche when those niches can live under the same umbrella business.

## Instagram Policy

- Niche Instagram accounts should use plus-tag aliases based on the umbrella brand and lane name.
- Example pattern: `imonengine+canvascurrentabstractart@gmail.com`
- Instagram is the preferred place to split niches beneath one umbrella brand.
- Keep each Instagram cluster to ten accounts or fewer per device or browser environment before moving to a fresh profile.

## Meta Delivery Note

- The Facebook Page remains live under the real Meta account.
- VPS browser login for Meta is optional once the Page API path is configured.
- Prefer `META_PAGE_ACCESS_TOKEN` + `META_PAGE_ID` for server-side Facebook posting.
